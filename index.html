About Me

Andrew Kulas is a senior at VCU and will be graduating December 2024. He is studying Financial Technology with a concentration in Financial Engineering.

He will be receiving a minor in Mathematics.

In 2020, Andrew received his associate degree in Engineering from Brightpoint Community College where he focused on Computer Science.

During summer 2023, Andrew worked with VCU's Computer Science and Electrical Engineering Department creating control scripts for autonomous control of aerial vehicles in their OpenCyberCity lab. During his time with VCU, he gathered technical programming skills along installing necessary hardware for scripts to run autonomously.
